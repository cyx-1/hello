# Gen Digital Inc. (GEN)

## Company Overview

**Gen Digital Inc.** (NASDAQ: GEN), formerly known as **NortonLifeLock Inc.** and **Symantec Corporation**, is a global cybersecurity company focused on consumer digital safety. The company powers Digital Freedom through a family of trusted consumer brands.

### Main Competitors

2. **Brand Portfolio**: Multiple trusted brands serving different market segments
3. **Freemium Model**: Avast and AVG drive user acquisition through free tiers
4. **Identity Protection**: LifeLock provides unique identity theft protection differentiator
5. **Bundling Strategy**: Combining antivirus, VPN, password manager, and identity protection
6. **AI Integration**: Norton Genie AI for fraud detection and protection
7. **Fintech Expansion**: MoneyLion acquisition extends into financial wellness

## Summary

Gen Digital Inc. has transformed from its origins as Symantec, an enterprise-focused security company, into the world's largest pure-play consumer cybersecurity company. Through strategic acquisitions including LifeLock and Avast, the company has assembled a portfolio of trusted brands serving over 500 million customers worldwide.

With consistent organic growth, strong margins, and expansion into fintech through the MoneyLion acquisition, Gen Digital is positioning itself as a comprehensive platform for digital and financial life management. The company's focus on AI-powered security, identity protection, and customer growth positions it well in the evolving cybersecurity landscape.
