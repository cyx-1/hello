# Avanade

## Company Overview

**Type:** Private Company
**Founded:** April 2000
**Headquarters:** Seattle, Washington, USA
**Ownership Structure:** Joint venture between Accenture (majority owner) and Microsoft (minority owner)
**Employees:** 50,000 (across 26 countries)
**Market Presence:** Serves 34% of the Fortune 500 and 46% of the Fortune Global 500

Avanade is the leading provider of digital, cloud and advisory services, sustainable industry solutions and design-led expertise across the Microsoft ecosystem. The company was formed as a joint venture between Andersen Consulting (later Accenture) and Microsoft, with Accenture increasing its stake from 50% to 78% in 2001.

## Ownership & Structure

- **Majority Owner:** Accenture (consolidated subsidiary since December 31, 2001)
- **Minority Owner:** Microsoft Corporation
- **Board Composition:** Five members drawn from leadership at Avanade, Accenture, and Microsoft
- **Public Reporting:** As of July 1, 2008, no longer required to file SEC reports after unregistering all securities
- **Operating Segments:** Three geographic business areas - Americas, Europe, and Asia Pacific

#### Cloud & Infrastructure Services

**Avanade Cloud Impact** - Five-module Azure journey framework:
1. **Plan:** Data-driven action plan with infrastructure analysis
2. **Build:** Accelerated data and app migration with automated discovery
3. **Move:** Smooth migration with integrated automation and monitoring
4. **Optimize:** Risk, cost, and sustainability management for AI-ready infrastructure
5. **Run:** 24/7 managed services with expert support

**ANALYZE Tool:**
- Self-service, fully automated cloud environment analysis
- Provides recommendations for cloud resource optimization
- Achieves 20% cost reduction on average (up to 50%)
- Zero cost for users

### Avanade's Differentiation Strategy

1. **Exclusive Microsoft Focus**
   - Only major consulting firm exclusively focused on Microsoft ecosystem
   - Joint venture structure with Microsoft ensures unique market position
   - Deepest Microsoft expertise in the industry

2. **Partnership Depth**
   - 19-time Microsoft Global SI Partner of the Year winner
   - More Microsoft certifications (60,000+) than any competitor
   - Client Zero status for new Microsoft technologies

3. **Scale & Expertise**
   - 50,000 employees dedicated to Microsoft solutions
   - Operates in 26 countries across all major markets
   - Combined with Accenture's 65,000 Microsoft-focused professionals

4. **Innovation Leadership**
   - First to market with new Microsoft technology implementations
   - Co-development of solutions with Microsoft
   - Leading Copilot and generative AI adoption

5. **Industry Recognition**
   - Serves 34% of Fortune 500 companies
   - Serves 46% of Fortune Global 500 companies
   - Consistent awards and recognition from Microsoft

**Competitive Positioning:** While competitors like Accenture, Capgemini, and TCS offer multi-vendor IT services, Avanade's exclusive Microsoft focus allows deeper expertise and faster time-to-value for Microsoft-centric enterprises. This specialization is both a strength (unmatched Microsoft expertise) and potential limitation (dependence on Microsoft ecosystem growth).
