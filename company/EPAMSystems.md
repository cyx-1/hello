# EPAM Systems Inc.

## Company Overview

EPAM Systems, Inc. (NYSE: EPAM) is a leading global provider of digital platform engineering and software development services. The company specializes in digital transformation services, software engineering, cloud services, data analytics, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity solutions.

Founded in 1993 by Arkadiy Dobkin and Leo Lozner in Princeton, New Jersey, with concurrent operations in Minsk, Belarus, EPAM has grown into a Fortune 1000 company and S&P 500 member. The company is headquartered in Newtown, Pennsylvania.

**Key Facts:**
- **Founded:** 1993
- **Headquarters:** Newtown, Pennsylvania, USA
- **IPO:** 2012 (NYSE: EPAM)
- **Employees:** ~61,700 (as of March 2025)
- **Market Cap:** ~$9.9 billion (as of November 2025)
- **CEO:** Balazs Fejes (effective September 1, 2025)
- **Executive Chairman:** Arkadiy Dobkin (Founder)

1. **Engineering-First Culture**
   - Born in product development with 30+ years of engineering execution
   - Strong focus on software craftsmanship and technical excellence
   - Higher percentage of senior engineers compared to competitors

2. **Digital Native Approach**
   - Cloud-native development as core competency
   - Strong GenAI and AI/ML capabilities
   - Platform engineering expertise

3. **Integrated Consulting + Delivery**
   - EPAM Continuum combines strategy, experience, and technology consulting
   - End-to-end delivery from concept to production
   - Faster time-to-market through integrated teams

4. **Strategic Cloud Partnerships**
   - Premier Partner status with Google Cloud, AWS, and Microsoft Azure
   - Deep expertise across all major cloud platforms
   - Award-winning AI and cloud solutions

5. **Vertical Expertise**
   - Strong domain knowledge in Financial Services, Healthcare, and Technology
   - Industry-specific accelerators and frameworks
   - Regulatory compliance expertise

6. **Geographic Delivery Model**
   - Diversified delivery centers across Europe, Latin America, and Asia
   - Nearshore capabilities for Americas clients
   - Strong presence in emerging tech hubs

### Market Position

EPAM is positioned as a premium digital engineering partner, differentiating from larger competitors through:
- Higher technical talent density
- Focus on complex, transformational engagements
- Stronger engineering culture vs. traditional consulting approach
- Agility and innovation speed of a smaller organization with scale of a global company
