## Main Products and Key Milestones

### Core Products
- **DC-to-DC Converter ICs** - Convert and control voltages in electronic systems
- **Power Modules** - Integrated power solutions up to 100A
- **Lighting Control ICs** - Backlighting for LCD panels
- **Class D Audio Amplifiers** - High-efficiency audio solutions
- **Motor Drivers** - For various applications

**Product Portfolio:** 13 product lines with over 4,000 products

### Key Milestones

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 1997 | Founded by Michael Hsing with vision to integrate power systems onto single chips |
| 2004 | IPO on NASDAQ (November) |
| 2013 | World's first Monolithic Power Module |
| 2021 | Added to S&P 500 (February) |
| 2023 | 426 issued patents |
| 2024 | 13th consecutive year of revenue growth; 1,700+ patents; 6th generation BCD process |

### Technology Differentiators
- **Proprietary BCD Process Technology** - Bipolar-CMOS-DMOS (6th generation)
- **55nm BCD Process** - Superior power density and efficiency
- **Fabless Model** - Focuses on design innovation

## Main Competitors and Differentiation

### Key Competitors

| Competitor | Revenue | Employees | Focus |
|------------|---------|-----------|-------|
| **Texas Instruments (TXN)** | $15.6B | ~34,000 | Broad analog/embedded processing |
| **Analog Devices (ADI)** | $9.4B | ~24,000 | High-performance analog/mixed-signal |
| **Infineon Technologies** | ~$16B | ~50,000 | Power semiconductors, automotive |
| **ON Semiconductor** | ~$8B | ~33,000 | Power, analog, sensors |
| **Power Integrations** | ~$500M | ~1,700 | AC-DC power conversion |

### MPS Competitive Differentiation

1. **Proprietary BCD Technology**
   - 6th generation Bipolar-CMOS-DMOS process
   - 55nm process node vs competitors' larger nodes
   - Higher power density and efficiency

2. **Fabless Business Model**
   - Lower capital requirements
   - Focus resources on R&D
   - Faster time-to-market

3. **High Integration Strategy**
   - Smaller form factor solutions
   - More functions on single chip

4. **Strategic Market Focus**
   - AI/Enterprise Data growth (121% YoY in 2024)
   - Automotive electrification

5. **Market Share Growth**
   - Analog market share: tripled to 2.5% over 9 years
   - Power semiconductor share: 2% (2017) to 4% (2022)
