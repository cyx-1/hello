## Products & Platform

### Core Platform: Full-Stack Generative AI

Writer provides an end-to-end platform for building, deploying, and supervising AI agents in the enterprise.

### Palmyra LLM Family (Proprietary Models)

| Model | Parameters | Release Date | Key Features |
|-------|-----------|--------------|--------------|
| Palmyra-Small | 128M | Feb 2023 | Lightweight tasks |
| Palmyra-Base | 5B | Feb 2023 | General purpose |
| Palmyra-Large | 20B | Feb 2023 | Complex reasoning |
| Palmyra-Instruct | - | Jan 2024 | 30 languages support |
| Palmyra-Vision | - | Feb 2024 | Multimodal (text + images) |
| Palmyra X 004 | - | Oct 2024 | #1 on Berkeley Tool Calling Leaderboard |
| Palmyra Creative | - | Dec 2024 | Creative writing focus |
| Palmyra X5 | - | Apr 2025 | 1M context window, adaptive reasoning, $1M training cost |

**Key Differentiator:** Training data is transparent and "distributed free of any copyright restrictions"

### Platform Components

#### 1. Knowledge Graph (May 2023)
- Graph-based Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Connects enterprise data sources to LLMs
- Customizable AI guardrails

#### 2. AI Studio (June 2024)
**Three Development Approaches:**
- **No-Code Builder:** Visual tools for business users
- **Writer Framework:** Open-source Python framework with visual editor
- **Writer API:** SDKs for Python and TypeScript

#### 3. Enterprise Features
- Style guide enforcement
- Brand voice consistency
- Regulatory and legal compliance
- Content templates (blogs, recaps, product descriptions)
- Audio/video summarization

### Compliance & Security
- SOC 2 Type II
- GDPR compliant
- HIPAA compliant
- PCI compliant
- On-premises deployment options

## Market Position & Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

| Company | Focus Area | Key Differentiator |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Jasper** | Marketing teams | Brand voice features, premium pricing ($49-$125/mo) |
| **Copy.ai** | Sales/GTM teams | Multi-model access (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini), $50/user/mo |
| **Cohere** | Enterprise AI | Cloud-agnostic deployment, on-premises options |
| **OpenAI** | General AI | Platform + killer apps, Azure integration |
| **Anthropic** | Enterprise AI | Safety-focused, 32% enterprise market share (Claude) |

### Writer's Competitive Differentiation

1. **Full-Stack Platform:** End-to-end solution vs. API-only providers
2. **Enterprise-First Design:** Built specifically for enterprise compliance and governance
3. **Transparent Training Data:** Copyright-free training data transparency
4. **Proprietary Models:** Palmyra family optimized for enterprise tasks
5. **High ROI:** Proven 333% ROI and 7.5 hours/week productivity gains
6. **Data Security:** Deploy within enterprise infrastructure
7. **Customization:** Models trained on company-specific data and brand guidelines
8. **Tool Calling Excellence:** #1 ranking on Berkeley Tool Calling Leaderboard

### Competitive Risks
- Large cloud providers (Microsoft Copilot, Google Gemini for Workspace) expanding into enterprise content
- OpenAI and Anthropic adding enterprise features
- Potential for feature replication by competitors
- Pricing pressure from well-funded alternatives
