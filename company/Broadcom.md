# Broadcom Inc. (AVGO)

## Company Overview

**Type:** Public Company (NASDAQ: AVGO)
**Headquarters:** Palo Alto, California, USA
**Founded:** 1961 (as HP Associates); rebranded as Broadcom Inc. in 2016 after Avago's acquisition of Broadcom Corporation
**CEO:** Hock Tan (since 2006)
**Industry:** Semiconductors, Infrastructure Software

Broadcom Inc. is an American multinational designer, developer, manufacturer, and global supplier of a wide range of semiconductor and infrastructure software products. The company serves the data center, networking, software, broadband, wireless, storage, and industrial markets.

## Main Products and Key Milestones

### Semiconductor Solutions
- **Networking:** Data center switching, routing, and network interface cards
- **Custom AI Accelerators:** Co-developed Google's TPU chips and Meta's MTIA chips
- **Broadband:** Cable modems, DSL, and PON solutions
- **Wireless:** WiFi, Bluetooth, and GPS solutions
- **Storage:** SAS/SATA controllers and enterprise SSD controllers

### Infrastructure Software (VMware + Legacy)
- **VMware Cloud Foundation:** Hybrid cloud platform
- **VMware vSphere:** Server virtualization
- **VMware NSX:** Network virtualization
- **Symantec Enterprise Security:** Endpoint and network security
- **CA Technologies:** Mainframe, DevOps, and automation software

### Key Milestones
- **November 2023:** Completed $61B acquisition of VMware
- **FY 2024:** AI revenue reached $12.2B (220% YoY growth)
- **FY 2024:** Adjusted EBITDA reached record $31.9B (37% YoY growth)
- **2024:** CEO Hock Tan joined Meta's board of directors

## Competitors and Differentiation

### Semiconductor Market Competitors

| Competitor | Differentiation |
|------------|-----------------|
| **Intel** | Focuses on CPUs and foundry services; Broadcom emphasizes custom silicon and networking |
| **Qualcomm** | Dominant in mobile; Broadcom leads in infrastructure and enterprise |
| **NVIDIA** | GPU/AI leader; Broadcom competes with custom AI accelerators for hyperscalers |
| **Marvell** | Data infrastructure; Broadcom has broader software portfolio |

### Infrastructure Software Competitors

| Competitor | Differentiation |
|------------|-----------------|
| **Microsoft (Hyper-V)** | Integrated with Azure; VMware offers multi-cloud flexibility |
| **Red Hat/IBM (KVM)** | Open source; VMware has enterprise support and ecosystem |
| **Nutanix** | HCI-focused; VMware has broader virtualization portfolio |
| **Oracle** | Database-centric; Broadcom focuses on infrastructure automation |

### Competitive Advantages
1. **Diversified Portfolio:** Both semiconductor and software revenues provide stability
2. **AI Custom Silicon:** Deep partnerships with hyperscalers for custom accelerators
3. **VMware Ecosystem:** ~375,000 companies run VMware solutions
4. **Operational Excellence:** Industry-leading margins under Hock Tan's leadership
5. **R&D Investment:** Significant IP portfolio across multiple technology domains

### Competitive Challenges
- VMware pricing changes have driven some customers to evaluate alternatives
- Competition intensifying in AI accelerator market from AMD and startups
