# Workday, Inc. (NASDAQ: WDAY)

## Company Overview

**Founded:** March 2005
**Founders:** Dave Duffield and Aneel Bhusri
**Headquarters:** Pleasanton, California
**IPO Date:** October 12, 2012 (NYSE, later moved to NASDAQ)
**Current CEO:** Carl Eschenbach (since March 2024)
**Executive Chair:** Aneel Bhusri
**Employees:** ~20,400 (as of November 2025)
**Market Cap:** ~$62 billion (as of November 2025)
**Customers:** Over 10,000 organizations worldwide (60%+ of Fortune 500)

### Company History

Workday was founded by Dave Duffield and Aneel Bhusri following Oracle's hostile takeover of PeopleSoft in December 2004. Duffield, the founder and former CEO of PeopleSoft, invested the initial $15 million to start the company. The company launched its first products in November 2006.

**Funding Timeline:**
- 2005: Initial $15M from Dave Duffield
- 2009: $75M funding led by New Enterprise Associates
- 2011: $85M funding (bringing total to $250M) - T. Rowe Price, Morgan Stanley, Bezos Expeditions
- 2012: IPO at $28/share, closed first day at $48.69

**Leadership Timeline:**
- 2009-2014: Co-CEOs Dave Duffield and Aneel Bhusri
- 2014-2020: CEO Aneel Bhusri
- 2020-2024: Co-CEOs Aneel Bhusri and Carl Eschenbach
- March 2024-Present: Sole CEO Carl Eschenbach

## Main Products & Milestones

### Workday Human Capital Management (HCM)

Core HR and talent management platform covering the entire employee lifecycle.

**Key Features:**
- Core HR and benefits administration
- Talent management and succession planning
- Recruiting and onboarding
- Learning management
- Compensation and payroll
- Time tracking and absence management
- Workforce planning and analytics

**Milestones:**
- **2006:** Initial HCM product launch
- **2014:** Acquired Identified for AI-driven talent intelligence
- **2021:** Acquired VNDLY for $510M (external workforce management)
- **Q3 FY2024:** Surpassed 5,000 core HCM customers
- **Q4 FY2025:** Over 6,100 core HCM and Financials customers
- **2024:** Workday Illuminate AI platform with AI agents for recruiting, succession

### Workday Financial Management

Enterprise financial management and accounting platform.

**Key Features:**
- General ledger and accounting
- Accounts payable/receivable
- Revenue management
- Asset management
- Financial consolidation
- Procurement
- Expense management
- Grants management
- Projects and project billing

**Milestones:**
- **2007:** Workday Financial Management launched
- **2018:** Acquired Adaptive Insights for planning capabilities
- **FY2024:** Record number of core FINS (Financial Management) wins
- **2024:** Record Financials wins in both Q4 and full fiscal year

### Workday Adaptive Planning

Enterprise planning, budgeting, and forecasting solution (formerly Adaptive Insights).

**Key Features:**
- Financial planning and analysis (FP&A)
- Workforce planning
- Operational planning (sales, demand, supply)
- Scenario modeling
- Rolling forecasts
- Close and consolidation
- Reporting and analytics
- AI/ML-powered predictive forecasting

**Milestones:**
- **2003:** Founded as Adaptive Insights
- **June 2018:** Acquired by Workday for $1.55 billion
- **August 2018:** Acquisition completed
- **May 2020:** Rebranded to Workday Adaptive Planning
- **2024:** Gartner Peer Insights Customers' Choice Award
- **Current:** ~7,000 customers worldwide
- **Average deployment time:** 4.5 months

### Workday Illuminate (AI Platform)

Next-generation AI capabilities announced at Workday Rising 2024.

**Key Features:**
- Generative AI for content creation (job descriptions, contracts)
- Workday Assistant for HR and finance task guidance
- AI orchestration and automation
- Four AI agents: Recruiter, Expenses, Succession, Workday Optimize
- AI Gateway for partner integrations

**Milestones:**
- **September 2024:** Announced at Workday Rising
- **2024:** Acquired Evisort (AI contract intelligence)

## Main Competitors & Differentiation

### SAP SuccessFactors

**Overview:** Cloud-based HCM solution from SAP, offering core HR, talent management, and analytics.

**Market Position:** Strong in global enterprises, especially those using SAP ERP

**Differentiation:**
- Best for multinational organizations with complex global requirements
- Superior integration with SAP S/4HANA and other SAP enterprise solutions
- Supports multiple languages, currencies, and tax systems
- Modular approach - companies can subscribe to relevant modules only
- Strong in emerging markets and complex compliance requirements
- Substantial investment in AI through SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP)
- Quarterly updates through continuous delivery model

**Target Customer:** Large global enterprises already invested in SAP ecosystem

---

### Oracle HCM Cloud

**Overview:** Comprehensive cloud HCM platform from Oracle, evolved from Oracle Fusion HCM.

**Market Position:** Enterprise-grade solution competing directly with Workday across HR and finance

**Differentiation:**
- Best alternative for user experience and configurability
- More comprehensive payroll capabilities across international jurisdictions
- Complete and integrated toolset for all HR processes
- Strong workforce modeling capabilities
- Integration with LinkedIn Talent Solutions
- Each module has separate pricing structure
- Extensive history in enterprise applications

**Target Customer:** Organizations seeking deep configurability and payroll flexibility

---

### ADP

**Overview:** Established provider known for payroll services, also offering full HCM suite.

**Market Position:** Market leader in payroll with extensive HR and benefits capabilities

**Differentiation:**
- Best alternative for analytics (ADP Vantage HCM)
- Decades of payroll expertise since 1949
- Strong compliance capabilities and regulatory expertise
- Robust benchmarking data from massive customer base
- ADP Workforce Now for midsize businesses
- ADP Vantage HCM for large enterprises needing strategic workforce management
- Flexible plans and tiers for different company sizes

**Key Stats:**
- Founded: 1949
- Employees (2021): ~58,000
- Revenue (2021): $15 billion

**Target Customer:** Organizations prioritizing payroll expertise and compliance

---

### Ceridian Dayforce

**Overview:** Global HCM platform with single-database architecture for unified HR, payroll, benefits, and workforce management.

**Market Position:** Growing challenger known for real-time processing and modern cloud architecture

**Differentiation:**
- Single-database architecture (vs. acquired/integrated modules)
- Real-time processing capabilities
- Up-to-the-minute data and calculations
- Unified application connecting all HR functions
- Over 6 million users worldwide
- Strong AI capabilities for workforce management
- Modern cloud-native experience
- Particularly strong in workforce management and scheduling

**Target Customer:** Organizations seeking unified platform with real-time data processing

### Competitive Positioning Summary

| Factor | Workday | SAP SuccessFactors | Oracle HCM | ADP | Ceridian Dayforce |
|--------|---------|-------------------|------------|-----|-------------------|
| **Best For** | Cloud-first enterprises | Global/SAP ecosystems | Configurability | Payroll/Compliance | Real-time processing |
| **Architecture** | Single cloud platform | Modular cloud | Modular cloud | Multiple products | Single database |
| **Deployment** | Cloud only | Cloud (SaaS) | Cloud | Cloud/Hybrid | Cloud |
| **Strength** | User experience, innovation | Global reach, SAP integration | Flexibility, payroll | Payroll expertise, data | Unified platform |
| **AI/ML** | Workday Illuminate | SAP BTP AI | Oracle AI | ADP Analytics | Dayforce AI |

### Selection Considerations

1. **Global Requirements:** Consider international presence, languages, currencies, tax systems
2. **Integration Landscape:** Evaluate existing technology ecosystem (Microsoft, SAP, Oracle)
3. **Implementation Capacity:** Workday requires significant internal resources; others may be more turnkey
4. **Company Size:** Larger enterprises benefit from SAP/Oracle; mid-market may prefer ADP/Ceridian
5. **Payroll Complexity:** ADP and Oracle have stronger global payroll; Workday partners for some markets
