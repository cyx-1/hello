## Main Products and Services

### Core Retail Operations
Walmart is the world's largest retailer with over 11,000 stores in 27 countries, offering:
- **Grocery & Food**: Fresh produce, packaged foods, beverages
- **General Merchandise**: Electronics, apparel, home goods, toys, sporting goods
- **Health & Wellness**: Pharmacy services, health products
- Over 140,000 items in stores
- 2.1+ million products online (including marketplace)

### Walmart+ Membership Service
**Launch Date**: September 15, 2020

**Pricing**: $98/year or $12.95/month (with 30-day free trial)

**Key Features**:
- Free unlimited delivery from stores ($35 minimum)
- Free shipping with no order minimum
- Fuel savings up to 10Â¢/gallon at 13,000+ stations (Exxon, Mobil, Murphy)
- Included Paramount+ or Peacock streaming subscription
- Walmart+ Travel: Earn up to 5% Walmart Cash on hotels, 2% on flights
- Scan & Go in-store checkout
- Free returns pickup from home

**Background**: Rebranded from Walmart's Grocery Unlimited service (launched 2019)

### Walmart Marketplace
**Current Scale (2025)**:
- 200,000+ active sellers
- 420+ million products available on Walmart.com
- 95% of products from third-party sellers
- 44,000 new sellers joined in first 5 months of 2025 (vs. 59,000 in all of 2024)
- 60% growth in 2024 to reach ~160,000 sellers

**Key Milestones**:
- **2024-2025**: Launched Premium Beauty and "Resold at Walmart" (pre-owned items marketplace)
- **April 2025**: Opened Cypress, Texas store with digital QR codes for extended marketplace
- **August 2024**: Announced category expansion and multichannel solutions for sellers

**Fulfillment Services**:
- Walmart Fulfillment Services (WFS): Storage, packing, shipping, returns
- Next-Day Delivery coverage in major cities (LA, NYC, Chicago, Houston, Atlanta)
- International marketplace operations in U.S., Canada, Mexico, Chile

## Main Competitors and Differentiation

### Amazon
**Differentiation Strategy**:
- Pure e-commerce focus with customer convenience and speed
- Amazon Prime: Fast delivery (1-2 days), personalized recommendations
- Robust logistics network with extensive fulfillment centers
- Lower inventory and distribution costs due to digital-first model
- Endless product assortment online

**Competitive Advantage**: Speed, convenience, and comprehensive online selection

### Target
**Differentiation Strategy**:
- "Affordable style" positioning with elevated shopping experience
- Partnerships with high-end brands and designers
- Exclusive product collaborations
- High-low pricing strategy (promotions + everyday pricing)
- Fashion-forward customer base focus
- Emphasis on store experience and design aesthetics

**Competitive Advantage**: Style and brand exclusivity at affordable prices

### Costco
**Differentiation Strategy**:
- Membership-based warehouse club model ($60-$120/year)
- Bulk purchasing for massive unit cost savings
- Kirkland Signature house brand for quality differentiation
- Limited SKU selection (~3,700 items)
- Low overhead costs enabling rock-bottom prices
- 90% membership renewal rate

**Competitive Advantage**: Bulk savings and membership loyalty

### Walmart's Differentiation
**Strategy**: Cost Leadership through Everyday Low Prices (EDLP)

**Competitive Advantages**:
- Enormous buying power from scale
- Advanced logistics and supply chain systems
- Operational efficiency and optimized labor costs
- Largest physical retail footprint (11,000+ stores)
- Hybrid model: Physical stores + e-commerce + marketplace
- Wide product assortment (140,000+ in-store items)

**Market Position**: Competes on price and convenience with broadest customer reach
