# Coinbase Global, Inc.

## Company Overview

**Status:** Public Company
**Stock Ticker:** COIN
**Exchange:** NASDAQ
**Founded:** June 2012
**Founders:** Brian Armstrong (CEO) and Fred Ehrsam
**Headquarters:** San Francisco, California, USA
**Industry:** Cryptocurrency Exchange & Financial Services

Coinbase is the largest U.S.-based cryptocurrency exchange platform with over 108 million verified users globally. The company went public via direct listing on NASDAQ on April 14, 2021, at an $86 billion valuation, marking a historic moment as the first major crypto exchange to have an IPO.

## Main Products & Services

### Core Trading Platform
- **Cryptocurrency Trading:** 250+ cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana
- **Stock Trading:** Launched December 2025 - hundreds of top stocks with plans for thousands more
- **Futures & Perpetuals:** 24/7 Bitcoin and Ethereum futures trading
- **Prediction Markets:** Partnership with Kalshi for real-world event trading (elections, sports, economics)

### Enterprise & Business Solutions

**Coinbase Business**
- Available in U.S. and Singapore
- Expanded API suite for custody, payments, trading, and stablecoins
- Reporting double-digit month-over-month growth

**Crypto-as-a-Service (CaaS)**
- Unified infrastructure and markets platform
- Tools for institutions to build scalable crypto products

**Coinbase Tokenize**
- Institutional stack for real-world asset tokenization

### Blockchain & Web3

**Base**
- Ethereum Layer-2 blockchain
- Available in 140+ countries globally
- Coinbase Wallet rebranded as "Base app" in November 2025

### Payment & Stablecoin Solutions

**Coinbase Payments**
- Stablecoin payments stack for commerce platforms
- Instant, 24/7 USDC payments globally

**Custom Stablecoins**
- Companies can create branded stablecoins backed by USDC
- Embedded rewards and seamless issuance managed by Coinbase

### Advisory Services

**Coinbase Advisor**
- AI-powered wealth management advisory service
- Launched December 2025

## Main Competitors

### Comparison Table

| Competitor | Key Differentiator | Fee Structure | Cryptocurrencies | Security Record |
|------------|-------------------|---------------|------------------|-----------------|
| **Binance** | World's largest by volume, 175+ countries | 0.1% (0.075% with BNB) | 600+ | Various incidents |
| **Kraken** | Advanced features, competitive pricing | 0% - 0.26% | 500+ | Never breached |
| **Gemini** | Regulatory compliance, conservative listings | 1.49% + up to 1% convenience fee | 100+ | Never breached |
| **Coinbase** | Largest U.S. exchange, user-friendly | 0.5% - 3.99% (varies) | 250+ | Strong record |

### Competitor Strategies

**Binance**
- **Differentiation:** Massive coin selection, lowest fees, global reach
- **Challenge:** Regulatory scrutiny in U.S. and multiple countries
- **Strategy:** Separated U.S. operations (Binance.US) with limited features

**Kraken**
- **Differentiation:** Advanced trading features, security, competitive pricing
- **Target:** Cost-conscious traders, professional/institutional clients
- **Strategy:** Focus on security and comprehensive trading options (futures, margin)

**Gemini**
- **Differentiation:** Regulatory compliance (NY Trust Company), institutional-grade security
- **Target:** Security-conscious investors, institutional clients
- **Strategy:** Conservative approach to listings, emphasis on compliance

**Coinbase Positioning:**
- Best for beginners with simple interface
- Strongest U.S. regulatory standing (publicly traded, S&P 500 member)
- "Everything Exchange" strategy expanding beyond crypto to stocks, prediction markets
- Institutional infrastructure through Coinbase Business and Prime
