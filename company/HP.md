# HP Inc. (HPQ)

## Company Overview

**Company Type:** Public (NYSE: HPQ)
**Headquarters:** Palo Alto, California, USA
**Founded:** November 1, 2015 (as HP Inc., split from Hewlett-Packard Company)
**Original Company Founded:** January 1, 1939 (Hewlett-Packard)
**Industry:** Information Technology, Personal Computers, Printers

HP Inc. is an American multinational information technology company that develops personal computers (PCs), printers and related supplies, as well as 3D printing solutions. It is the world's second-largest personal computer vendor by unit sales after Lenovo and ahead of Dell as of 2024.

## Main Products and Key Milestones

### Personal Computer Portfolio

#### Consumer PC Lines
- **Essential/Pavilion** - Entry-level and mainstream consumer laptops/desktops
- **Envy** - High-end consumer laptops with premium features
- **Spectre** - Ultra-premium, prosumer laptops
- **OMEN** - Gaming laptops, desktops, and accessories (introduced May 2016)
- **OmniBook/OmniDesk/OmniStudio** - AI-powered consumer PCs

#### Business PC Lines
- **HP Pro Series** - Small business computers
- **HP Elite Series** - Enterprise-class laptops and desktops

## Main Competitors and Differentiation Strategies

### Personal Systems Competitors

| Competitor | Key Strengths | Differentiation Strategy |
|------------|---------------|-------------------------|
| **Lenovo** | Global #1 in PCs; strong ThinkPad brand; competitive pricing | Focus on emerging markets; enterprise reliability; comprehensive product range |
| **Dell Technologies** | Direct sales model; strong enterprise relationships | Customer-centric approach; robust enterprise solutions; supply chain efficiency |
| **Apple** | Premium brand; ecosystem integration; design excellence | Vertical integration; macOS differentiation; premium pricing |
| **Acer** | Competitive pricing; gaming focus (Predator) | Value proposition; emerging market presence |
| **ASUS** | Gaming expertise (ROG); innovation | Design innovation; gaming peripherals ecosystem |

### Printing Competitors

| Competitor | Key Strengths | Differentiation Strategy |
|------------|---------------|-------------------------|
| **Canon** | Imaging expertise; quality reputation | Innovation in imaging technology; professional photo printing |
| **Epson** | EcoTank ink system; visual communications | Tank-based ink systems; sustainability focus; diverse product lines |
| **Brother** | Reliability; business focus | Compact designs; business-oriented features; service support |
| **Xerox** | Enterprise printing; managed print services | Large-format printing; document management solutions |

### HP's Differentiation Strategy

**Key Strategic Pillars:**

1. **AI Innovation Leadership**
   - Investing heavily in AI-powered PCs and workflows
   - HP AI Companion software integration
   - OMEN AI for gaming optimization
   - First quantum-resistant printers

2. **Brand & Product Portfolio Breadth**
   - Comprehensive range from entry-level to premium
   - Strong brand equity in both consumer and enterprise
   - Market-leading positions in both PCs and printers

3. **Cost Management & Efficiency**
   - Targeting $1.9 billion in savings by end of FY2025
   - Operational excellence initiatives

4. **Sustainability Commitment**
   - Environmental sustainability initiatives
   - Recycling programs for cartridges
   - Energy-efficient product designs

5. **Subscription & Services**
   - HP Instant Ink subscription service
   - Managed print services
   - Device-as-a-Service offerings

6. **Channel Partner Ecosystem**
   - Strong partner relationships (HP Amplify program)
   - Global distribution network

### Competitive Dynamics

- **PC Market:** Intense competition on price and features; HP differentiates through design, AI capabilities, and brand trust
- **Printing Market:** HP's dominant 34% market share provides scale advantages; competition focuses on total cost of ownership and sustainability
- **Key Risk:** Declining print market and shift to digital; mitigated by expanding supplies business and subscription models
