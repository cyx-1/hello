# Accenture plc (NYSE: ACN)

## Company Overview

Accenture plc is a global professional services company headquartered in Dublin, Ireland, providing consulting, technology services, and operations solutions. As one of the world's largest consulting firms, Accenture combines unmatched experience and specialized capabilities across more than 40 industries, powered by the world's largest network of Advanced Technology and Intelligent Operations centers.

**Fiscal Year End:** August 31

**Employees:** ~774,000 (end of FY2024)

## Main Services and Milestones

### Service Lines

**Consulting Services**
- Strategy & Consulting
- Technology transformation advisory
- Digital strategy and implementation
- Enterprise transformation
- Mergers & acquisitions support

**Technology Services**
- Cloud services (Cloud First initiative)
- Data & AI implementation
- Enterprise platforms (SAP, Oracle, Microsoft, Salesforce)
- Cybersecurity
- Application development and modernization

**Operations (Managed Services)**
- IT outsourcing (data centers, networks, help desks)
- Business process outsourcing (HR, finance, customer service)
- Industry-specific outsourcing (insurance claims, supply chain)
- Infrastructure management

**Accenture Song** (formerly Accenture Interactive)
- Digital marketing
- Brand experience
- Customer engagement
- Creative and content services

### Key Milestones

**1953 - Origins**
- Arthur Andersen's administrative services division designed and implemented the first automated payroll system using a UNIVAC I computer at GE's Appliance Park

**1989 - Separation from Arthur Andersen**
- Began operating independently as Andersen Consulting

**2001 - IPO and Rebranding**
- Formally separated from Arthur Andersen
- Rebranded as "Accenture" (Accent on the Future)
- Completed IPO
- Revenue: $6.9 billion

**2020 - Cloud First Launch**
- Invested $3 billion to create a 70,000-strong Cloud First division
- Strategic partnerships with AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud

**2022 - Accenture Song Launch**
- Renamed Accenture Interactive to Accenture Song
- Emphasized digital marketing, brand experience, and customer engagement

**2023 - AI Investment Announcement**
- Announced $3 billion investment in AI over three years
- Target of 80,000 AI practitioners by FY2026

**2024 - Generative AI Momentum**
- GenAI bookings reached $3 billion for full fiscal year
- Acquired Udacity as part of $1 billion LearnVantage initiative
- $6.58 billion in acquisitions throughout FY2024

**2025 - Reinvention Services Reorganization**
- Announced consolidation of five divisions (Strategy & Consulting, Technology, Operations, Industry X, Song) into Reinvention Services
- Effective September 1, 2025
- GenAI revenue tripled year-over-year to $2.7 billion

## Main Competitors and Differentiation

### Big Four Consulting

**Deloitte**
- **Focus:** Digital transformation, risk management, audit/tax integration
- **Differentiation:** Comprehensive service range combining consulting with audit and tax; strong regulatory compliance expertise
- **Advantage vs. Accenture:** Broader professional services ecosystem; strong in regulated industries

### Technology-Focused

**IBM Consulting**
- **Focus:** AI (Watson), cloud computing, blockchain, legacy system modernization
- **Differentiation:** Proprietary technology integration (Watson AI, Red Hat, IBM Cloud); deep technical expertise
- **Advantage vs. Accenture:** Can bring own technologies to implementations; strong in complex enterprise infrastructure

### India-Centric IT Services

**Cognizant**
- **Focus:** Industry-specific solutions in healthcare, financial services, retail
- **Differentiation:** Deep domain expertise; Neuro suite for automation and AI; competitive pricing
- **Advantage vs. Accenture:** More cost-effective; specialized industry knowledge; strong digital operations

**Infosys**
- **Focus:** Cloud adoption (Infosys Cobalt), data analytics, IoT, banking solutions
- **Differentiation:** Strong engineering heritage; R&D investments; academic partnerships
- **Advantage vs. Accenture:** Innovation through Finacle banking suite; cost-competitive delivery model

**TCS (Tata Consultancy Services)**
- **Focus:** Scalable global delivery, emerging market presence, cost optimization
- **Differentiation:** Extensive global footprint; local market knowledge; BaNCS platform
- **Advantage vs. Accenture:** More cost-effective for specific IT needs; strong in Asia Pacific and emerging markets

### Competitive Position

- **Accenture Market Position:** Undisputed leader in IT Services, twice as large as next competitor
- **Key Differentiators:**
  - Strategic technology integration with broader business goals
  - Largest global delivery network
  - $3B+ AI investment and 55,000+ data/AI practitioners
  - Strong partnerships with hyperscalers (Microsoft, AWS, Google, NVIDIA)
  - Industry-leading bookings ($81B+ in FY2024)
