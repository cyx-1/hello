# ServiceNow, Inc. (NOW)

## Company Overview

**Type:** Public Company (NYSE: NOW)
**Headquarters:** Santa Clara, California, USA
**Founded:** 2003 as Glidesoft, Inc. by Fred Luddy; incorporated 2004; renamed Service-Now.com in 2006
**CEO:** Bill McDermott (since November 2019)
**Industry:** Enterprise Software, IT Service Management, Workflow Automation

ServiceNow is a cloud computing company that provides digital workflow software for enterprise organizations. The company's Now Platform enables organizations to automate and manage digital workflows across IT, employee, customer, and creator experiences.

| Competitor | Overlap Area |
|------------|--------------|

| **Microsoft** | Power Platform, Teams integration |

### Competitive Advantages

1. **Platform Breadth:** IT, employee, customer, creator workflows on one platform
2. **AI Leadership:** Gartner Magic Quadrant Leader for AI in ITSM
3. **Enterprise Focus:** Deep relationships with large organizations
4. **Consistent Growth:** 20%+ revenue growth for 10+ years
5. **Bill McDermott Leadership:** Proven enterprise software CEO
6. **Innovation Pace:** Aggressive AI and automation roadmap

### Competitive Challenges

- High cost and complexity for smaller organizations
- Implementation requires significant investment
- Competition from platform bundlers (Microsoft, Salesforce)
