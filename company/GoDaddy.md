# GoDaddy Inc. (GDDY)

## Company Overview

**Stock Symbol:** GDDY (NYSE)
**Headquarters:** Tempe, Arizona, USA
**Founded:** 1997 (as Jomax Technologies)
**CEO:** Aman Bhutani (since 2019)
**Industry:** Internet Services & Infrastructure
**Website:** [godaddy.com](https://www.godaddy.com)

GoDaddy is the world's largest domain registrar and a leading web hosting company, serving over 20 million customers globally. The company manages over 82 million domains, accounting for approximately 20% of all domains worldwide.

## Company History and Key Milestones

### Founding and Early Years (1997-2005)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 1997 | Founded as Jomax Technologies by Bob Parsons in Phoenix, Arizona |
| 1999 | Renamed to GoDaddy after "Big Daddy" domain was unavailable |
| 2000 | Received ICANN accreditation for international domain registration |
| 2001 | Achieved first profit |
| 2004 | Sold nearly 7 million domain names; became world's leading registrar |
| 2005 | Launched domain auction marketplace; became largest ICANN-accredited registrar |

### Growth and Expansion (2006-2015)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 2006 | Officially rebranded to GoDaddy (February) |
| 2011 | Private equity acquisition (KKR, Silver Lake, TCV) for $2.25B (70% stake) |
| 2012 | International expansion began with India operations |
| 2013 | Reached 10 million customers; $1 billion annual profit; Blake Irving became CEO |
| 2015 | IPO on NYSE (April 1); stock soared 30% on first day; $4.5B valuation |

### Recent Developments (2017-Present)

| Year | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 2017 | Scott W. Wagner appointed CEO (December 31) |
| 2019 | Aman Bhutani appointed CEO |
| 2020 | Acquired Neustar domain registry services (renamed GoDaddy Registry) |
| 2021 | Relocated corporate headquarters to Tempe, Arizona (April) |
| 2024 | Achieved $4.57B annual revenue; outlined 2024-2026 growth strategy |
| 2025 | On track for $4.86B-$4.94B revenue; $1.5B+ free cash flow target |

### Key Acquisitions

- **Wild West Domains (2003):** Expanded domain portfolio
- **Host Europe Group (HEG):** Enhanced European hosting capabilities
- **Sucuri:** Added cybersecurity and web security services
- **Neustar Domain Registry (2020):** Added .co, .us, .biz, .club TLDs

### Main Competitors

#### 1. Squarespace
**Differentiation Strategy:**
- Premium, design-focused templates targeting creatives and professionals
- Superior e-commerce features with detailed product management
- Advanced SEO tools and comprehensive analytics
- All-in-one solution without reliance on third-party apps
- Higher price point for professional features

**Target Market:** Creative professionals, designers, bloggers, small businesses seeking premium aesthetics

#### 2. Wix
**Differentiation Strategy:**
- Highly customizable drag-and-drop editor
- Extensive app marketplace (500+ third-party integrations)
- Large template library
- ADI (Artificial Design Intelligence) for automated website creation

**Target Market:** Users seeking maximum customization and flexibility

#### 3. Namecheap
**Differentiation Strategy:**
- Competitive pricing on domain registration
- Privacy protection included by default
- Simple, straightforward interface
- Strong focus on domain management

**Target Market:** Cost-conscious users primarily focused on domain registration

#### 4. WordPress.com / Bluehost
**Differentiation Strategy:**
- Open-source platform with maximum flexibility
- Vast plugin ecosystem
- Full control over code and hosting
- Preferred by developers and technical users

**Target Market:** Developers, technical users, and those needing custom solutions

### GoDaddy's Competitive Advantages

1. **Market Leadership:** Largest domain registrar globally (55M+ .com domains)
2. **Brand Recognition:** 20+ years in the industry with strong brand awareness
3. **Price Competitiveness:** Most affordable website builder starting at $10.99/month
4. **Ease of Use:** Simplified setup for quick website deployment
5. **Comprehensive Ecosystem:** One-stop-shop for domains, hosting, email, and marketing
6. **AI Innovation:** GoDaddy Airo for AI-powered website creation
7. **24/7 Customer Support:** Award-winning customer service with GoDaddy Guides
8. **Scale:** Serves 20+ million customers; manages 82+ million domains (20% of all domains worldwide)
