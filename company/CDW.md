# CDW Corporation

## Company Overview

**Company Type:** Public
**Stock Ticker:** CDW (NASDAQ)
**Founded:** 1984
**Headquarters:** Vernon Hills, Illinois, USA
**Employees:** ~15,100
**Market Cap:** ~$20.6 billion (as of October 2025)
**Industry:** Information Technology Solutions and Services

CDW Corporation is an American multi-brand provider of information technology services, serving business, government, education, and healthcare sectors across the United States, the United Kingdom, and Canada. Originally founded as MPK Computing by Michael Krasny, the company later became Computer Discount Warehouse before adopting its current name in 2003. CDW is a Fortune 500 company and a member of the S&P 500 and Nasdaq-100 indices.

## Main Competitors and Differentiation Strategies

### Primary Competitors

| Competitor | Key Characteristics | Differentiation Strategy |
|------------|--------------------|-----------------------------|
| **SHI International** | Global IT solutions provider | Focus on industry-specific customization; 35+ offices worldwide; 1,000+ technical resources; strong software and cloud focus |
| **Insight Enterprises** | Fortune 500 solutions integrator | Microsoft US Partner of the Year; pivot to Systems Integrator model; strong enterprise consulting |
| **PC Connection** | Traditional VAR model | Larger product catalog (300,000+ items); higher inventory levels; competitive pricing |
| **Dell Technologies** | Direct manufacturer/seller | Vertical integration; direct-to-customer model; hardware manufacturing advantage |
| **HP Enterprise** | IT infrastructure focus | Enterprise hardware focus; strong R&D capabilities |

### CDW's Competitive Advantages

1. **Diverse Portfolio:** Comprehensive product and service offerings across hardware, software, and services
2. **Vendor Partnerships:** Strong relationships with 250+ technology vendors
3. **Market Presence:** Nearly 40 years of experience and established customer relationships
4. **Multi-Segment Coverage:** Ability to serve corporate, public, small business, government, education, and healthcare sectors
5. **Geographic Reach:** Operations in US, UK, and Canada
6. **Brand Trust:** Fortune 500 status and S&P 500/Nasdaq-100 membership

### CDW's Challenges

- More limited international presence compared to some competitors
- Revenue decline trends in recent years
- Competition from direct manufacturers and cloud-native providers
