| **Industry** | Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) |

Jabil Inc. is a U.S.-based multinational manufacturing company providing engineering, manufacturing, and supply chain solutions. The company was founded by James Golden and William Morean in 1966, starting by building circuit boards on a kitchen table in Detroit, Michigan. Originally named Jabil Circuit, Inc., the company changed its name to Jabil Inc. in June 2017.

Today, Jabil operates over 100 locations across 30 countries and is the world's third-largest manufacturing solutions provider.

## Business Segments

Jabil operates through three primary business segments:

### 1. Intelligent Infrastructure
- **Focus**: AI, cloud computing, data centers, networking, and communications
- **Performance**: Surged 51-62% YoY in FY2025
- **Revenue Share**: ~44% of total revenue (FY2025)
- **Key Drivers**: AI/ML compute, networking products for hyperscale customers

### 2. Regulated Industries
- **Focus**: Automotive, healthcare, defense, and renewable energy
- **Key Services**: Medical devices, electric vehicle components, aerospace systems
- **Notable**: Jabil is the largest healthcare manufacturing solutions provider globally

### 3. Connected Living & Digital Commerce
- **Focus**: Consumer electronics, digital print, retail solutions
- **Key Services**: Product design, manufacturing, and fulfillment for consumer devices

| **Sanmina** | USA | High-complexity manufacturing, quality focus | Smaller global footprint, high operational costs |
| **Benchmark Electronics** | USA | Specialized in complex products | Smaller scale |

### Jabil's Competitive Differentiation

**Strengths:**
1. **Global Scale**: 100+ locations in 30 countries with 135,000+ employees
2. **Diversified End Markets**: Aerospace, automotive, healthcare, AI/cloud, consumer electronics
3. **Vertical Integration**: Complete product lifecycle from design to aftermarket service
4. **AI/Data Center Leadership**: Strong positioning in AI infrastructure with ~44% revenue from Intelligent Infrastructure
5. **Technical Capabilities**: Design engineering, material sciences, thermal management, additive manufacturing
6. **Healthcare Leadership**: Largest healthcare manufacturing solutions provider globally
7. **Financial Performance**: Consistent core margin improvement (5.4-5.5%) and strong cash flow generation

**Strategic Focus Areas:**
- AI-powered cloud data centers
- Electric vehicles and renewable energy
- Healthcare and medical devices
- Supply chain resilience and reshoring

### Industry Dynamics
- EMS industry characterized by high-volume, low-margin operations
- Increasing focus on AI servers driving competition for high-value contracts
- Geopolitical tensions between US-China affecting supply chain strategies
- Growing emphasis on supply chain diversification and reshoring

## Summary

Jabil Inc. has successfully transformed from a traditional electronics manufacturing services company into a strategic AI infrastructure leader. The divestiture of the lower-margin Mobility business in 2023 and subsequent focus on AI data centers, healthcare, and regulated industries has positioned the company for sustained growth. With AI-related revenue expected to reach $11.2 billion in FY2026 and continued margin expansion, Jabil represents a compelling story in the secular growth of AI infrastructure.
