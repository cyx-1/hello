## Main Products and Business Segments

### 1. Semiconductor Test (70%+ of Revenue)

The core business providing automated test equipment for integrated circuits.

**Key Product Families:**
- **J750** - Entry-level test platform
- **UltraFLEX** - High-performance SoC testing
- **FLEX** - Flexible test platform
- **Eagle** - Power management IC testing (acquired 2008)
- **Magnum/Magnum EPIC** - Memory testing

**Applications:** Testing of logic, RF, analog, power, mixed-signal, and memory devices

**Key Customers:** Samsung, Qualcomm, Intel, Analog Devices, Texas Instruments, IBM

**Market Position:** ~30-35% global market share in semiconductor test equipment

### 2. Robotics/Industrial Automation (~15% of Revenue)

**Universal Robots (UR)** - Collaborative Robots (Cobots)
- Acquired in 2015 for $285 million
- Market leader with 40-50% cobot market share
- Over 75,000 cobots sold worldwide
- Product lineup: UR3e, UR5e, UR10e, UR16e, UR20, UR30
- Applications: Machine tending, packaging, assembly, quality inspection
- Industries: Automotive, electronics, food & beverage, pharma

**Mobile Industrial Robots (MiR)** - Autonomous Mobile Robots
- Acquired in 2018 for $148 million (+ $124M earnout)
- Handles internal logistics for payloads under 1,500 kg
- Headquartered in Odense, Denmark

**Other Robotics:**
- **AutoGuide** - High-payload autonomous mobile robots (acquired 2019)
- **Energid** - Robot control software (acquired 2018)

### 3. System Test

Defense/aerospace, storage device testing, and semiconductor production board test

### 4. Wireless Test (LitePoint)

Test solutions for WiFi, Bluetooth, GPS, 5G, and cellular devices

## Competitive Landscape

### Semiconductor Test Equipment Market

Teradyne and Advantest form a **duopoly with ~95% combined market share**.

| Company | Market Position | Headquarters | Key Strengths |
|---------|----------------|--------------|---------------|
| **Teradyne** | #1 or #2 | USA | Strong in SoC and RF testing; diversified with robotics |
| **Advantest** | #1 or #2 | Japan | Strong in memory testing; established in Asia |
| **Cohu** | Distant #3 | USA | Handler equipment; acquired Xcerra |

### Teradyne's Competitive Differentiation

1. **Diversification into Robotics**
   - Unique among test equipment peers
   - ~15% revenue from industrial automation
   - Hedge against semiconductor cyclicality

2. **Technology Leadership**
   - Strong R&D pipeline
   - Advanced probe card IP partnerships (Technoprobe investment: $516M)
   - AI-driven analytics for test optimization

3. **Customer Relationships**
   - Embedded with top semiconductor manufacturers
   - Large installed base driving service revenue

4. **AI & HBM Positioning**
   - Well-positioned for AI chip testing surge
   - Magnum 7H for High Bandwidth Memory
   - Data center and compute test demand

5. **Geographic Balance**
   - More geographically diversified than Advantest
   - ~40% China/Taiwan exposure vs ~60% for Advantest

## Investment Thesis

### Bull Case
- AI-driven semiconductor testing demand acceleration
- HBM and advanced packaging driving test complexity
- Robotics business providing growth and diversification
- Strong market position in duopoly
- Projected EPS doubling from 2024 to 2028

### Bear Case
- Cyclicality of semiconductor industry
- China/Taiwan geopolitical exposure
- Robotics business slower growth than expected
- Customer concentration risk

### Analyst Outlook
- Rating: "Moderate Buy" consensus
- Earnings growth: 21.6% per year projected
- Revenue growth: 12.5% per year projected
- 2028 Revenue target: $4.5-5.5 billion
