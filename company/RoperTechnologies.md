# Roper Technologies Inc.

## Company Overview

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Company Type** | Public |
| **Stock Symbol** | ROP (NASDAQ) |
| **Founded** | 1981 (original company founded 1890) |
| **Headquarters** | Sarasota, Florida, USA |
| **Employees** | ~18,200 |
| **Market Cap** | ~$51.5 billion |
| **Index Membership** | NASDAQ 100, S&P 500, Fortune 1000 |

Roper Technologies is a diversified technology holding company that designs and develops software and engineered products and solutions. The company operates a decentralized business model where each portfolio company operates independently. Roper positions itself as a "free cash flow compounder," whereby excess free cash flow generated by its portfolio businesses is repatriated to the parent company, which is then utilized to acquire additional businesses. The company currently operates approximately 30 distinct businesses with over three-fourths of revenue coming from software products and over two-thirds from recurring and reoccurring sources.

### Competitive Differentiation Strategies

#### Roper Technologies
- **Decentralized operating model:** Portfolio companies operate independently with autonomy
- **Software-focused transformation:** Shifted from industrial manufacturing to high-margin software
- **Niche market leadership:** Targets market-leading positions in specialized verticals
- **Cash flow compounding:** Reinvests strong FCF into disciplined acquisitions
- **Recurring revenue focus:** >67% of revenue from recurring/reoccurring sources
- **Low capital intensity:** Minimal capex requirements enable higher free cash flow conversion

#### Danaher Corporation
- **Danaher Business System (DBS):** Proprietary continuous improvement and lean manufacturing methodology
- **Integration approach:** Actively transforms acquired companies using DBS
- **Life sciences concentration:** Heavy focus on healthcare and diagnostics

#### Fortive Corporation
- **Fortive Business System (FBS):** Adapted from Danaher's playbook
- **Industrial technology focus:** Maintains more traditional industrial exposure
- **Connected devices:** IoT and industrial automation emphasis

#### AMETEK Inc.
- **Growth Model:** Operational excellence, organic sales growth, efficiency improvements
- **Patent portfolio:** 3,800 global patents protecting technological advantages
- **Disciplined M&A:** 14 acquisitions since 2020 totaling ~$1.4B annualized sales

#### IDEX Corporation
- **Specialized fluid handling:** Deep expertise in precision pumps and metering
- **Decentralized structure:** Similar autonomous business unit model
- **Niche market focus:** Targets defensible market positions

### Roper's Competitive Advantages

1. **Business Model Excellence:** Outperformed S&P 500 by 3.3x over the past decade
2. **Superior Cash Generation:** Consistent 30%+ free cash flow margins
3. **Acquisition Track Record:** 50+ acquisitions since 2000 with strong returns
4. **Software Transformation:** Successfully pivoted from industrial to technology company
5. **Sticky Customer Relationships:** Mission-critical software creates high switching costs
6. **Management Quality:** Experienced team with strong capital allocation discipline
