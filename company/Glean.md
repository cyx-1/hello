# Glean - AI Search Company Research

## Company Overview

**Type:** Private Company
**Founded:** 2019
**Headquarters:** Palo Alto, California
**Current Valuation:** $7.2 billion (June 2025)
**Total Funding Raised:** $765 million
**Employees:** 850+ globally

Glean Technologies, Inc. is an enterprise-grade AI-powered search and work assistant platform that integrates with workplace applications to help employees find information, generate content, and automate workflows.

## Main Products and Key Features

### Core Product: Glean Work AI Platform

**Primary Capabilities:**
1. **AI-Powered Enterprise Search** - Natural language understanding across 100+ workplace applications
2. **Glean Assistant** - AI chatbot for answering questions and generating content
3. **Glean Agents** - Autonomous AI agents for automating workflows and tasks
4. **Enterprise Graph** - Personalized knowledge graph for each user

**Key Features:**
- Contextual answers and content summarization
- Multi-step task execution and workflow automation
- Personalized search results based on user role, past searches, and workflows
- Security and permissions-aware search
- Report, email, and presentation generation
- Integration with 100+ enterprise applications

## Main Competitors and Differentiation

### Direct Competitors

| Competitor | Strengths | Glean's Differentiation |
|------------|-----------|------------------------|
| **Microsoft Copilot/Viva** | Deep Microsoft 365 integration (Outlook, Word, Excel, Teams) | Cross-platform integration (100+ apps vs. Microsoft-only); Superior personalization via Enterprise Graph; Independent of cloud ecosystem |
| **Google Cloud Search** | Native Google Workspace integration (Gmail, Drive, Docs, Sheets, Calendar) | Multi-platform support beyond Google; Better AI-powered understanding; Stronger enterprise features |
| **Perplexity AI** | Combines internal data with external web sources; Real-time internet insights | Focused enterprise search across internal repositories; Stronger security and permissions; Better workplace integration |
| **Guru** | Strong knowledge management and structured content | Superior AI-powered semantic search; Better automation through agents; More comprehensive integrations |
| **Elastic Search** | Powerful search infrastructure; Developer-focused | User-friendly interface; Pre-built enterprise integrations; AI-native design |
| **Coveo** | Strong analytics and insights capabilities | Better natural language understanding; More advanced AI agents; Superior personalization |
| **Dashworks** | Lower cost ($12/month, no minimum seats) | Enterprise-grade features; Better AI capabilities; Stronger Fortune 500 presence |
| **Lucidworks** | E-commerce and customer-facing search | Internal enterprise focus; Better employee productivity features |

### Glean's Key Differentiators

1. **Personal and Enterprise Graph Technology**
   - Individual-level personalization based on role, activity, conversations, projects
   - Context-aware across entire enterprise data landscape

2. **AI Agent Platform**
   - 30+ pre-built agents for automation
   - Natural language agent creation
   - Multi-step task execution

3. **Platform-Agnostic Integration**
   - 100+ workplace app integrations
   - Not locked to single ecosystem (Microsoft, Google, etc.)
   - Unified search across all enterprise tools

4. **Industry-Leading Engagement**
   - 40% wDAU/MAU ratio (2-4x higher than competitors)
   - 5 queries per user per day

5. **Enterprise-Grade Security**
   - Permissions-aware search
   - On-premises deployment option
   - SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA compliance

6. **Superior AI Technology**
   - Founded by ex-Google Search and Maps engineers
   - Advanced natural language understanding
   - Semantic search with deep learning

### Competitive Weaknesses (Areas for Improvement)

- **Pricing:** More expensive than alternatives like Dashworks (2-3x higher)
- **Collaboration Features:** Limited compared to dedicated knowledge management tools
- **AI Memory:** Session-based like many competitors, though Personal Graph addresses this

### Market Position

- **Target Market:** Enterprise customers (500+ employees) willing to pay premium for best-in-class AI search
- **Sweet Spot:** Technology companies, financial services, and large enterprises with complex app ecosystems
- **Go-to-Market:** Direct sales for large enterprises; Partner channels for geographic expansion

## Summary

Glean has established itself as a leader in enterprise AI search, achieving remarkable growth from founding in 2019 to a $7.2B valuation in 2025. The company's combination of world-class AI technology (from ex-Google/Facebook engineers), strong customer engagement metrics (40% wDAU/MAU), and rapid revenue growth ($100M ARR in 3 years) positions it well in the growing enterprise AI market.

Key strengths include:
- Superior personalization through Enterprise and Personal Graph technology
- Platform-agnostic approach with 100+ integrations
- Strong enterprise customer base (200+ customers including Fortune 500)
- Innovative AI agent platform for workflow automation
- Well-funded with $765M raised from top-tier investors

The company faces competition from tech giants (Microsoft, Google) and specialized players, but differentiates through its independent, AI-native platform with best-in-class search and personalization capabilities. With strong backing, proven product-market fit, and expanding international presence, Glean is well-positioned for continued growth in the enterprise AI market.
