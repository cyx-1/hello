# Enphase Energy Inc.

## Company Overview

**Stock Ticker:** NASDAQ: ENPH
**Headquarters:** Fremont, California
**Founded:** March 2006
**IPO:** March 2012
**Industry:** Solar Energy Technology / Clean Energy

Enphase Energy, Inc. is a global energy technology company and the world's leading supplier of microinverter-based solar and battery systems. The company designs, develops, manufactures, and sells home energy solutions that manage energy generation, energy storage, and control and communications on one intelligent platform.

### Key Statistics
- Shipped approximately 84.8 million microinverters
- More than 5.0 million Enphase-based systems deployed
- Operations in over 160 countries
- Market Cap: ~$4.0 billion (as of November 2025)

## Products & Services

### Core Products

#### Microinverters

**IQ8 Series Microinverters**
- Industry's first grid-forming microinverters
- Split-phase power conversion capability
- Proprietary ASIC chip built on 55nm technology
- 25-year limited warranty
- Can operate in grid-tied or off-grid modes

**IQ8P Microinverters**
- Peak output power of 480W
- Designed for high-powered modules up to 670W DC
- Available globally including Europe, Asia-Pacific

#### Energy Storage

**IQ Battery**
- All-in-one AC-coupled storage system
- Total usable energy capacity up to 10.08 kWh
- Multiple embedded grid-forming microinverters
- 3.84 kW power rating
- Available in multiple sizes (IQ Battery 5P)

#### EV Charging

**IQ EV Charger 2** (Launched 2025)
- Smart charger integrated with solar and battery systems
- Solar-optimized charging starting at 1.38 kW
- AI optimization with forecasting algorithms
- Available in Europe, Australia, New Zealand, U.S.

**IQ Bidirectional EV Charger** (Expected 2026)
- Vehicle-to-home (V2H) and vehicle-to-grid (V2G) support
- DC port connection to EV

#### Software & Services

**Enlighten Platform**
- Cloud-based monitoring service
- Real-time energy monitoring and control
- Mobile app for homeowners
- Design and proposal tools for installers

**IQ Gateway & IQ Energy Router**
- Communications hub for system management
- Grid services enablement

### Manufacturing

- U.S. manufacturing facilities in Texas
- "DOM" suffix products qualify for Domestic Content Bonus Credit
- Contract manufacturing in Asia

## Competitive Landscape

### Market Position

Enphase and SolarEdge together control approximately 90% of the U.S. residential solar inverter market:
- **Enphase:** ~50% market share
- **SolarEdge:** ~40-45% market share

### Main Competitors

| Competitor | Technology | Differentiation |
|------------|------------|-----------------|
| **SolarEdge Technologies** | String inverters with power optimizers | Lower cost per watt; strong commercial segment |
| **Tesla Energy** | String inverters, Powerwall | Brand recognition; vertical integration with EVs |
| **SunPower Corporation** | Integrated solar systems | Premium residential market; full-service offering |
| **Huawei Solar** | String inverters | Strong in European/Asian markets; price competitive |
| **Fronius International** | String and hybrid inverters | Strong in commercial; Austrian manufacturing |

### Enphase Competitive Advantages

1. **Technology Leadership:** Microinverter technology provides module-level optimization and monitoring
2. **Reliability:** Over 1 million cumulative hours of power-on testing; industry-leading 25-year warranty
3. **Installer Loyalty:** 74% of U.S. installers carry Enphase (vs. 60% for SolarEdge)
4. **Integrated Ecosystem:** Solar, storage, and EV charging on single platform
5. **Domestic Manufacturing:** U.S. production helps customers qualify for IRA incentives
6. **Safety:** No high-voltage DC on roof; reduced fire risk

### Market Challenges

- High interest rate environment reducing residential solar demand
- European market softening due to falling electricity prices
- Net metering policy changes reducing solar economics
- Increased competition from Tesla and Chinese manufacturers
