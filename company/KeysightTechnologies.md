# Keysight Technologies Inc.

## Company Overview

**Ticker Symbol:** NYSE: KEYS
**Headquarters:** Santa Rosa, California, USA
**Founded:** November 1, 2014 (spin-off from Agilent Technologies)
**Employees:** ~15,000
**Market Cap:** ~$29-31 billion
**Industry:** Electronics Test and Measurement Equipment

Keysight Technologies, Inc. is an American company that manufactures electronics test and measurement equipment and software. The company was formed as a spin-off of Agilent Technologies, which inherited and rebranded the test and measurement product lines developed and produced, from the late 1960s to the turn of the millennium, by Hewlett-Packard's Test and Measurement division.

## Business Segments

Keysight operates through two primary business segments:

### 1. Communications Solutions Group (CSG)
- **FY 2024 Revenue:** $3.42B (69% of total revenue)
- Serves commercial communications and aerospace, defense, and government markets
- Provides electronic design and test software, instruments, and systems
- Key areas: 5G/6G, wireless communications, network testing, aerospace & defense

### 2. Electronic Industrial Solutions Group (EISG)
- **FY 2024 Revenue:** $1.56B (31% of total revenue)
- Serves general electronics, semiconductor, and automotive markets
- Provides solutions for manufacturing, R&D, and quality assurance
- Key areas: Automotive/EV testing, semiconductor testing, general electronics

| **Viavi Solutions** | Network test | Network visibility, fiber optics |
| **Spirent Communications** | Network performance testing | Now acquired by Keysight |

### Market Position
- **#4 in Oscilloscopes** with 11.7% mindshare
- Only electronic design and measurement company in the S&P 500
- Market leader in 5G test equipment
- Strong position in aerospace, defense, and government markets

### Keysight's Competitive Advantages

1. **Technology Foresight:** 2-3 year lead time in developing technology stack; early to innovations in 5G-Advanced and 6G
2. **Breadth of Portfolio:** Over 3,600 solutions with 30,000+ product combinations
3. **End-to-End Solutions:** Complete communications network testing from data center to mobile devices
4. **R&D Investment:** Increased from 12% to 16% of revenue (2014-2020)
5. **Software Leadership:** Strong EDA and test automation software ecosystem
6. **Strategic Acquisitions:** Ixia, Spirent, ESI Group enhance capabilities across markets

### Differentiation Strategy
- Award-winning oscilloscopes with fastest update rates and deepest memory
- Industry's only capacitive touchscreen on oscilloscopes
- Largest selection of oscilloscope probes
- Multiple instruments in one mainframe
- Easy upgrade paths for lowest total cost of ownership
- Strong focus on emerging technologies: AI, 6G, EV/AV, quantum computing
