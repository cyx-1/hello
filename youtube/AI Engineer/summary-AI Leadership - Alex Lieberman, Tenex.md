## Executive Summary

This is the opening segment of the AI Engineer Code Summit 2025, hosted by Alex Lieberman, co-founder of Morning Brew and current co-founder of Tenex. The video captures the conference welcome, where Lieberman introduces himself and explains his journey from newsletter publishing to AI transformation consulting. He engages with the audience from various locations (New York, San Francisco, Austin, Ecuador, and New Zealand) and acknowledges the event sponsors before transitioning into the main conference programming.
