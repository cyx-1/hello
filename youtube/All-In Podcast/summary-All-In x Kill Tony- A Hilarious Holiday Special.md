# All-In x Kill Tony: A Hilarious Holiday Special

**Video URL:** https://youtu.be/7_3gKoQPUfM

## Executive Summary

This is a recording of the All-In Podcast's second annual holiday party, featuring Tony Hinchcliffe from Kill Tony as a special guest replacing Chamath. The event combines comedy roasting, the "Bestie Awards" ceremony, and a live Kill Tony comedy set. The hosts (Jason Calacanis, David Sacks, and David Friedberg) participate in roasts, discuss their 2024 predictions and outcomes, announce award winners, and engage in humorous banter about tech, politics, and current events. The atmosphere is casual and comedic, blending Silicon Valley insider culture with stand-up comedy.
